Australia

Countries covered

A remote yet highly developed country, Australia is diverse and beautiful, offering a range of breathtaking natural landscapes, vibrant cities and its Aboriginal culture that has been preserved for over 50,000 years!. Attracting about six million visitors each year, Australia is known for its unique natural wonders, exotic wildlife, cultural richness, stunning beaches, and a range of adventurous and thrilling activities. It is one travel destination that can cater to every type of traveler and is therefore immensely popular across nationalities and age-groups!

The Best of Australia

Beaches and
Adventure Sports

With a coastline that stretches over 23,000 miles, Australia has some of the best beaches in the world! There are more than 11,000 beaches across the country, including big wave surfer havens, white sand beaches, and family-friendly ones. You can soak in the sun or partake in one of the many water sports at any of these stunning beaches such as the Bondi Beach in Sydney and Surfers Paradise in the Gold Coast. There is also the iconic natural wonder, the Great Barrier Reef, which is the largest coral reef system in the world and is the only living organism visible from space! You can try your hand at snorkeling or diving as well as kayaking and wave surfing while in this country. Other activities that you can indulge in and feed your adventurous spirit include rock climbing, hiking and trekking in the Blue Mountains, snow skiing in Victoria or New South Wales, mountain biking in Canberra, hot air ballooning in Brisbane, Canberra or the red Centre, among others.

Bustling
Cities

Australia is also famous for its vibrant cities that are sprinkled between mountains, rivers, and wide-open plains, offering a unique blend of art, culture, and architecture. The country’s oldest and the largest city which is known for its picturesque harbour, Sydney, offers the most cosmopolitan ambiance with its lively nightlife and vibrant culture. Meanwhile Melbourne is the cultural capital with its European influence and its famous food and shopping scene. The capital city of Canberra is another exciting destination known for its architecture and museums. On the other hand, Adelaide is the wine capital of the world and Brisbane is renowned for its sandy beaches.

Australia Highlights

Driving along the Great Ocean Road in Australia is a breathtaking journey that takes you along the stunning coastline of Victoria. It's one of the world's most scenic coastal drives, offering dramatic ocean views, lush rainforests, and iconic landmarks.

Scuba diving in the Great Barrier Reef is an extraordinary experience that allows you to explore one of the world's most renowned and biodiverse underwater ecosystems. Watch in awe as graceful sea turtles glide through the water, and keep your eyes peeled for reef sharks patrolling the depths.

Kuranda is a picturesque village located in the rainforest of Far North Queensland, Australia. It's nestled in the midst of the World Heritage-listed Wet Tropics Rainforest, which is one of the oldest and most diverse rainforests on the planet.
